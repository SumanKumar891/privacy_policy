Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [29-05-2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how Cloud Sense Viz (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, we may access certain device features to deliver the app's functionality.

2. Permissions and Their Purpose

  • INTERNET – Required to access network resources such as Firebase, OpenStreetMap, and push notifications.
  • WAKE_LOCK – Keeps the device awake during critical operations like background tasks.
  • VIBRATE – Enables vibration feedback for user notifications.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – Restarts scheduled tasks after device reboot.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – Allows certain services to continue running while the app is in the background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Enables sending of notifications to the user.
  • CAMERA – Used for scanning QR codes to add a device by scanning its ID.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services which may collect information according to their privacy policies:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – For delivering push notifications.
  • Flutter Local Notifications – For showing local scheduled notifications.
  • OpenStreetMap – For providing map tiles and geocoding services to display device locations.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not store your personal data. Any data handled is processed securely through third-party services like Firebase and OpenStreetMap.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

6. User Control

Users can manage permissions through their device settings. Disabling certain permissions may limit some app functionality.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page.
